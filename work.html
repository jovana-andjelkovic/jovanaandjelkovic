Selected work

2018-2019

Design system for complex B2B fintech

I led the implementation of TradeCore's first design system to improve consistency, speed up delivery, and give engineering and design a shared language. I reviewed the existing codebase to ground the specs, ran research to validate decisions, and sequenced the rollout so engineering could move in parallel. It was adopted across our existing product suite and became the foundation for every new one.

2019

Reducing complexity in data querying and filtering

Filters were tightly coupled to column visibility, making them unusable on data-heavy tables. The query builder had a related issue where unclear condition grouping was driving consistent Customer Success requests. I used observation and research to ground the redesign of both and worked with engineering to ship them. Requests related to querying dropped significantly within the first few months.

2022

Discovery and search at a high-traffic B2C travel platform

GetYourGuide attracts around 25 million monthly visitors. I worked on two projects shaping how they discover and search activities, using data analysis and user research to inform decisions alongside the wider team. The work improved the user journey and contributed to a positive impact on global conversion rate. It also deepened my understanding of consumer search behaviour and directly inspired a filter vision initiative I went on to drive.
